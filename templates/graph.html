I Gotchu!

To do any kind of selection on the graph, you must first activate "Selection Mode" by clicking on the button at the top right corner of the page.

For Nodes:

  • To edit a node, press and hold E on your keyboard, then click on the node you want to edit.
  • The node's key/value and name will appear in the input boxes to the left.
  • Do not edit the node's name to update the value of that node. Otherwise, you'd be creating a new node.

For Edges:

  • To select a source node, press and hold S on your keyboard, then click on the node you wish to be the source.
  • To select a target node, press and hold T on your keyboard, then click on the node you wish to be the target.
  • Finally, hit the "Add Node" button and bam, new edge.

For Delete:

  • To select a node to delete, press and hold D on your keyboard, then click on the node you wish to delete
  • Then, hit the "Delete Node" button (make sure the selected node under -Node is the node you clicked) and bam, byebye node.
